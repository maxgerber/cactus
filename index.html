We recognise that the struggle to continue in an age of global uncertainty can be all-consuming.

We take our name from the family of plants which have adapted through millenia to be able to withstand extreme draught by conserving large reserves of water.

the cactus is a symbol of hope in troubled times.

  • To economic migrants and refugees, undocumented or documented, hounded, moved like cattle across borders and scapegoated by a system which blames them for its inherent flaws.
  • To those hit hardest by cuts to mental health services. It's called illness because it can be terminal.
  • To fighters for justice in a society upholding racial hierachy and then camoflauging its efforts.

We salute you.
